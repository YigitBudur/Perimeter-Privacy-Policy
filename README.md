## Privacy Policy for Perimeter

**Effective Date:** [25.11.2025]

At Shield Arc, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our game, Perimeter.

### 1. Information We Collect

**To provide you with cloud-saving features and a consistent gameplay experience, we collect specific data related to your game progress. By registering for or playing Perimeter, you acknowledge and consent to the collection and storage of the following:**

* **Gameplay & Account Data:** We collect your **Google Play Services Player ID** (a unique identifier provided by Google) and your **gameplay data** (such as level progress, inventory, scores, and settings). This data is stored securely using **Firebase Firestore**, a cloud-hosted database.
* **Advertising Identifiers:** We use ad tracking technologies, such as the Advertising ID on Android and Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) on iOS, to serve personalized ads.
* **Device Information:** Non-personal data about your device may be collected to improve ad performance and relevance, such as your device type, operating system, and app usage statistics.

**We do not collect traditional personally identifiable information (PII) such as your real name, physical address, email address, or phone number.**

### 2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

* **Game Functionality & Cloud Storage:** Your **Player ID and gameplay data are stored in Firebase Firestore** to allow you to save your progress, retrieve your game data across different devices, and maintain your user profile within the game.
* **Personalized Ads:** Ad networks may use the collected advertising identifiers to show you personalized ads based on your interests.
* **Ad Performance:** Device data is used to ensure ads are relevant and functioning correctly.

### 3. Third-Party Partners & Data Storage

We use third-party services to support our game infrastructure and advertising. These partners may collect, store, and use information as described above.

**Data Storage Provider:**
* **Firebase Firestore (Google):** We use Firebase Firestore to store your game data and Player ID. You can read more about how Google handles data in their privacy policy.

**Advertising Partners:**
* [Google Play Services](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)
* [AdMob](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en)
* [Google Analytics for Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

### 4. Children's Privacy

Our game is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete such information promptly.

### 5. Your Choices

**Ads:**
You can opt out of personalized ads by changing the settings on your device:
* On Android: Go to "Settings" > "Google" > "Ads" and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization."
* On iOS: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy" > "Advertising" and enable "Limit Ad Tracking."

**Game Data:**
If you wish to have your **gameplay data and Player ID removed from our Firebase Firestore database**, please contact us at the email provided below. Note that deleting this data will result in the loss of your game progress.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this section with an updated effective date. Continued use of the game after any such changes signifies your acceptance of the new terms.
